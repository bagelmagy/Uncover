About Uncover

Uncover is a location-based social discovery app that helps you explore your surroundings and connect with people nearby. Whether you're looking to make new friends, discover local events, or simply see what's happening around you, Uncover makes it easy and fun.

Our mission is to help people break out of their digital bubbles and engage with the real world around them. We believe that meaningful connections happen when people discover shared interests in shared spaces.
